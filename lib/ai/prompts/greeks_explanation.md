# Greeks Explanation Prompt

Explain the options Greeks for the current position or chain.

## Delta
- Measures price sensitivity to underlying movement
- Range: -1 to +1 for individual options
- Interpretation: probability proxy for ITM at expiration

## Gamma
- Rate of change of delta
- Highest for ATM options near expiration
- Risk: gamma exposure can cause rapid P&L swings

## Theta
- Time decay per day
- Negative for long options, positive for short
- Accelerates as expiration approaches

## Vega
- Sensitivity to implied volatility changes
- Higher for longer-dated options
- Important for volatility trading strategies

## Rho
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Generally less significant for short-term options
- More relevant for LEAPS and long-dated positions
