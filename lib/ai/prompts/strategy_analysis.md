# Strategy Analysis Prompt

Analyze the {{strategy}} strategy for the given options chain.

## Analysis Framework

### Entry Criteria
- Optimal strike selection
- Timing considerations
- Market conditions favoring this strategy

### Risk/Reward Profile
- Maximum profit potential
- Maximum loss exposure
- Break-even points

### Greeks Exposure
- Delta: Directional exposure
- Gamma: Rate of delta change
- Theta: Time decay impact
- Vega: Volatility sensitivity

### Risk Management
- Key risks specific to this strategy
- Adjustment techniques
- Exit criteria and stop-loss considerations

### Position Sizing
- Capital allocation recommendations
- Portfolio impact considerations
